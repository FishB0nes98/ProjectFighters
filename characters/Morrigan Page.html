Morrigan Character Bio

Stats

  • AD: 54 (+3/lvl) -> 129
  • AP: 0 (+0/lvl) -> 0
  • HP: 593 (+305/lvl) -> 7933
  • Mana: 300 (+55/lvl) -> 1620
  • MS: 333 (+4/lvl) -> 421
  • AS: 0.52 (+0.032/lvl) -> 0.944

Abilities

Passive: Succubus Wrath

Morrigan has 14% Spellvamp from the beginning of the game.

Q: Hate Spike

[40 mana] [3s cd]

Morrigan summons a spike at the cursor's location on the ground. If it hits an enemy, it deals [70/125/250/385/485] damage and slows them by [30%] for (3s). If Morrigan hits the same enemy again within (5s), it deals [180%] of the ability's base damage, until 5s pass or Morrigan doesn't hit the target again.

W: Sharp Shield

[100 Mana] [21s/18s/16s/14s/13s cd]

Morrigan places a thorny shield on herself, which explodes after (2s) or if Morrigan is auto-attacked by an enemy. The shield can be canceled by Morrigan (up to 2s). When the shield is destroyed, all nearby enemies take [80/150/350/500/600] damage and block one basic attack.

E: Bat Dash

[85 Mana] [12s cd]

Morrigan dashes in the targeted direction. If Morrigan passes through an enemy during the ability, it charms them for (0.8s)..

R: Spike Garden

[100 Mana] [110s cd]

Morrigan charms all enemies around her for (1s) and deals [675/1000/1100] damage to them. Already charmed enemies take [50%] more damage.

Talents

Level 1

  • a. Morrigan's Q ability slows for an additional [10%] and lasts for [3s] instead of 2s.
  • b. After using the Bat Dash ability, Morrigan becomes untargetable for [0.65] second, during which she cannot be targeted by abilities.
  • c. Morrigan gains 5% damage reduction for [2s] if she hits an ability. This can goes up to 15%.

Level 5

  • a. Morrigan's Sharp Shield now blocks 50% of the damage she would receive first from a spell and it still blocks an autoattack.
  • b. Morrigan's maximum health increases by 22%, but all her abilities deal only 60% damage.
  • c. Morrigan's abilities have slightly increased range.

Level 10

  • a. The charm duration of Morrigan's R ability increases from 1s to [1.5s].
  • b. If Bat Dash was used within (2s) before using the R ability, Bat Dash's cooldown resets to 0.

Level 15

  • a. Morrigan ignores half of the enemy's magic resistance with her first spell hit (20 seconds cd/target).
  • b. QUEST: Do not die until level 15 -> Morrigan receives an activatable item that allows her to teleport to any point on the map (60s cooldown).
  • c. Double Q hit silences the enemy for [0.5s].

Level 20

  • a. Morrigan's Spellvamp increases from 15% to [25%].
  • b. Enemies charmed by Morrigan take 20% more damage.
  • c. Morrigan gains 50% bonus movement speed if she is below 40% HP for [3s].
  • d. Ultimate Succubus form: All of Morrigan's abilities deal 15% more damage, but her Spellvamp is reduced to 5%.
