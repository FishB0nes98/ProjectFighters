Sophitia - The Holy Warrior

Stats

  • AD: 50 (+4.5/lvl) -> 158
  • HP: 622 (+339/lvl) -> 8758
  • Mana: 311 (+80/lvl) -> 2231
  • MS: 320 (+3.5/lvl) -> 404
  • AS: 0.43 (+0.055/lvl) -> 1.75

Abilities

Passive: Divine Blessing

Sophitia gains bonuses based on her current HP:

  • 100-95% HP: +5/12/20/28/35 AD
  • 94%-80% HP: +3/4/5/6/8 Mana regen/s
  • 79%-50% HP: Reduces crowd control time by 0.2/0.3/0.35/0.5/0.7 seconds

Q: Holy Strike

[90 Mana] [12/11/10/9/8s cd]

Sophitia swings her sword twice in front of her. Each hit deals [45/100/165/275/350 + 40% AD] damage. If the target is affected by the bleed from her W ability, Sophitia heals for [50%] of the damage dealt and refreshes the bleed duration.

W: Sting

[90 Mana] [15s/13s/10s/9s/9s cd]

Sophitia stabs a nearby enemy with her sword, immediately slowing them by [80%] for [3.5s] and dealing [10/14/20/28/33] damage every [0.5s] during the slow.

E: Shield of Faith

[80 Mana] [20/17/17/15/14s cd]

Sophitia increases her movement speed by [20%] and raises her shield in a defensive stance, creating a large protective wall in front of her that absorbs all incoming damage for [3s]. When the wall expires, it sends out a wave that stuns all enemies in front of her for [1.5s]. Expiration can be earlier if the ability is pressed again. During this ability, Sophitia cannot use her Q and W abilities.

R: Prayer

[0 Mana] [190/160/140s cd]

Sophitia's HP regeneration increases to [240/380/530] per second for [5s].

Talents

Level 1

  • a. Holy Warrior: Increase the bonuses from Sophitia's passive by 20%. Additionally, her attack speed increases by 0.2 when her health is above 95%.
  • b. Divine Strike: Sophitia's Q ability now also reduces the cooldown of her W by 1 second per hit. Additionally, hitting an enemy with Q restores 5% of her maximum mana.
  • c. Guardian's Path: Sophitia's E ability also grants a shield to nearby allies and herself, absorbing 20% of the damage she absorbs for [2s].

Level 5

  • a. Aegis Mastery: Reduces the cooldown of E by 3 seconds and increases the duration of the defensive wall by 1 second.
  • b. Valorous Might: Increases Sophitia's basic attack damage by 15% and grants 5% lifesteal.
  • c. Blessed Recovery: Increases the effectiveness of healing received by 25% when Sophitia's health is below 35%.

Level 10

  • a. Divine Endurance: Increases the duration of Sophitia's R ability by 2 seconds and grants her 30% damage reduction while it is active.
  • b. Sacred Renewal: While R is active, Sophitia radiates an aura that heals nearby allies for 15% of the healing she receives from her R.

Level 15

  • a. Sharp Edge: Sophitia's Q ability now causes enemies to bleed for an additional 50% of the damage dealt.
  • b. Persistent Will: Reduces the cooldown of all abilities by 0.5 second every time Sophitia hits an enemy with her basic attacks.
  • c. Blessing of Athena: Using any ability grants Sophitia a shield that absorbs damage equal to 5% of her maximum health for 5 seconds.

Level 20

  • a. Divine Intervention: When Sophitia falls below 14% health, she becomes immune to spells for 2 seconds. This effect has a 120-second cooldown.
  • b. Retribution Strike: Sophitia's Q now strikes three times instead of two and the healing from the bleed effect is increased to 65% of the damage dealt.
  • c. Sanctified Barrier: Defensive Wall reflecting the negated damage to the attacker by [5%] of the negated damage.
  • d. Celestial Empowerment: All of Sophitia's abilities gain an additional effect:
    • Q: Hitting an enemy reduces their armor by 3% for 3 seconds.
    • W: The slow and bleed effect lasts an additional 1 second.
    • E: Sophitia is immune to CC while holding the shield.
