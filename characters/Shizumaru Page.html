Abilities

Passive: Fly-by

Whenever Shizumaru is knocked up into the air, he gains the following bonuses:

  • Shizumaru can move and use spells while knocked up.
  • Shizumaru deals 40% more damage for 1.5 seconds after the knock-up ends.
  • Shizumaru can cancel the knock-up with abilities.
  • Shizumaru takes no damage from spells while being knocked up.

Q: Slice Forward

[80 Mana] [18/16/13/12/11s cd]

Shizumaru strikes with his blade and dashes in the targeted direction. If he passes through enemies, he deals [95/200/390/520/710] damage and gains [8/18/30/42/55] AD for 3 seconds. During Dash, Shizumaru opens his umbrella forward, and negate any spell damage that comes from the direction he is facing. This ability can be used while in the air and cancels the knock-up.

W: Self Fly-by

[50 Mana] [23/18/17/16/14s cd]

Shizumaru jumps into the air using his umbrella, remaining airborne for [1.5s].

E: Blade Blending

[60 Mana] [8s cd]

Shizumaru releases a shockwave in a targeted short direction. If it hits an enemy, it deals [50/90/165/220/370] magic damage, slows them by [40%] for [1.5s], and interrupts any jump abilities.

R: Spinning Slasher

[100 Mana] [75s cd]

Shizumaru targets a nearby enemy, opens his umbrella, and continuously chases and spins around the target for [4.5s]. The ability deals [55/75/105] damage every [0.5s]. During the ability's duration, W (Self Fly-by) can be used, allowing Spinning Slasher to continue, but no other abilities can be used. While Fly-by or Self Fly-by is active, Spinning Slasher deals no damage. The ability can be interrupted at any time by Shizumaru or by a stun.
