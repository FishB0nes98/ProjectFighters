Nina - The Sniper Assassin

Stats

  • Damage: 68 (+10/lvl) -> 308
  • HP: 589 (+145/lvl) -> 4069
  • Mana: 452 (+25/lvl) -> 1052
  • MS: 360 (+3.6/lvl) -> 446
  • AS: 0.72 (+0.037/lvl) -> 1.61

Abilities

Passive: Dual Gun

Nina starts with a bonus pistol in her inventory. When activated, her next basic attack will deal damage with both pistols. [30/27/21/17/11s cooldown]

Q: Sniper Shot

[80 mana] [21/18/17/16/12s cd]

Nina draws her sniper and fires in a long, narrow direction. If it hits an enemy, it deals [105/200/415/600/995] damage. If the enemy is below 10% HP, it executes them. Sniper Shot counts as an auto attack.

W: Mark of The Assassin

[100 Mana] [20s cd]

Nina marks an enemy. She moves 25% faster towards this enemy and places a mark on them. If she hits this enemy with 3 basic attacks within 5 seconds, the third attack deals [90/155/355/510/895] bonus damage and she gains 5 bonus AD for the rest of the match.

E: Sudden Assault

[50 Mana] [5s cd]

Nina tumbles in the targeted direction. If she collides with an enemy, she stuns them for [1.35s] and deals [50% AD] damage.

R: Aid

[100 Mana] [225s cd]

Nina calls a helicopter to her current location, which arrives after 1 second. Re-activating this ability makes Nina jump onto the helicopter, allowing her to choose a drop-off point on the map. If no point is chosen, Nina lands at the base after 3.5 seconds. When Nina jumps onto the helicopter, she restores [100% HP and 100% Mana].
