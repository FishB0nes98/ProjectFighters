Abilities

Passive: Beast Gauge

Yugo has a Beast Gauge that fills up when dealing damage(+3), receiving damage(+7), and killing minions(+1) scoring takedowns(+20). The gauge can be filled to a maximum of 100 points. Transforming into wolf form consumes any points of the gauge, with the duration of the transformation proportional to the amount of the gauge consumed (0.5 seconds per 1 points). Yugo gains 2% lifesteal in human form and 5% lifesteal in wolf form.
