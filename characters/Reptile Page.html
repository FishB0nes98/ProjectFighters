Abilities

Passive: Poisoned Casting

Reptile's every hit ability or basic attack deals additional [80-555] damage over 5 seconds (max 3 stacks).

Q: Acid Spit

[70 mana] [6s cd]

Reptile spews a large amount of acid in the targeted direction, dealing [125/230/440/780/930/1225] damage. It creates a pool of acid at the end of the ability's range or behind hit enemies, which slows enemies by [55%] and triggers the passive. The poison spreads among enemies nearby the hit target, resetting the duration of the poison.

W: Exuviation

[50 Mana] [13s/12s/11s/10s/10s cd]

Reptile sheds its slimy skin, removing all crowd control effects.

E: Fadeaway

[- Mana] [10s cd]

Reptile becomes invisible to enemies for [5/7/8/9/10] seconds. Instead of bonus damage, it increases Reptile's overall damage by [20%] for [3s] after breaking invisibility.

R: The Mark of The Lizard

[100 Mana] [80s cd] [1.5s casting time]

Targets an enemy, placing a mark on them for [15s]. While the mark is active, Reptile runs towards them at double speed and deals [130%/175%/200%] bonus damage. The mark ignores enemy shields for its duration.
