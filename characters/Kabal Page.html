Talents

Level 1

  • a. Lethal Hooks: Meat Hook now deals 100% of Kabal’s AD as damage when pulling an enemy and the slow is increased to 50%.
  • b. Swift Winds: Kabal's movement speed is increased by an additional 10%, and his basic attacks grant 10% bonus movement speed for 2 seconds (stacking up to 3 times).
  • c. Tactical Retreat: Kabal runs [20%] faster if there are no enemies nearby.

Level 5

  • a. Extended Chains: Increases the range of Meat Hook by 20%.
  • b. Spinning Fury: The stun effect lasts [0.5s] longer.
  • c. Quick Hands: Speedy Hands now also grants 8% increased attack damage for its duration, and the attack speed bonus lasts 1 second longer.

Level 10

  • a. Toxic Mist: Hide in Gas now poisons enemies who enter the smoke, dealing 1.25% of their maximum health as magic damage per second for the duration of the smoke.
  • b. Vanishing Act: Hide in Gas's cooldown is reduced by 20 seconds, and the first attack out of stealth deals 50% bonus damage.

Level 15

  • a. Iron Grip: Meat Hook now roots the target for 1.5 seconds after the pull.
  • b. Blender Mode: Spinner Winner can now be recast within 2 seconds to dash again, but it can’t stun enemies anymore.
  • c. Overdrive: Speedy Hands now doubles Kabal’s attack speed instead, however Kabal loses 20% of his AD at LVL15.

Level 20

  • a. Unstoppable Force: Kabal gains a magic shield, which negates the first CC and its damage [35s cooldown].
  • b. Reaper's Hooks: Each enemy hooked increases Kabal’s attack damage by 15% for 5 seconds.
  • c. Gas Cloud: Hide in Gas now creates a larger smoke cloud that follows Kabal’s movements, affecting a wider area and lasting 2 seconds longer. Enemies inside the smoke have their vision greatly reduced.
  • d. Vortex Slash: Spinner Winner now creates a vortex that pulls nearby enemies towards Kabal during the spin.
