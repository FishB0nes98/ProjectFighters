[65 mana] [8s cd]

[50 Mana] [13s cd]

Cham Cham's lifesteal increases by [8%] for [4s].

E: Flicky Trick

[40 Mana] [10s cd]

Cham Cham jumps in the designated direction, able to jump over walls. If her Q ability hits an enemy, she can jump towards that enemy from a great distance.

R: Huge Boomerang

[100 Mana] [65s cd]

Cham Cham launches a wide and short-range boomerang in the designated direction, dealing [300/510/650/790] damage to all enemies hit, slowing them by [35%], and marking them for [3s]. The Q ability goes on cooldown at the start of the cast. This ability can only be cast if Cham Cham is holding the boomerang.
