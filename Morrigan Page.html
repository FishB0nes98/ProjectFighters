Abilities

Passive: Succubus Wrath

Morrigan has 15% Spellvamp from the beginning of the game.

Q: Hate Spike

[40 mana] [3s cd]

Morrigan summons a spike at the cursor's location on the ground. If it hits an enemy, it deals [90/160/285/445/530] damage and slows them by [30%] for (2s). If Morrigan hits the same enemy again within (5s), it deals [150%] of the ability's base damage, until 5s pass or Morrigan doesn't hit the target again.

W: Sharp Shield

[90 Mana] [13s cd]

Morrigan places a thorny shield on herself, which explodes after (2s) or if Morrigan is auto-attacked by an enemy. The shield can be canceled by Morrigan (up to 2s). When the shield is destroyed, all nearby enemies take [100/200/400/800/995] damage and block one basic attack.

E: Bat Dash

[85 Mana] [12s cd]

Morrigan dashes in the targeted direction. If Morrigan passes through an enemy during the ability, it charms them for (0.6s) and deals [50/90/155/220/300/400] damage.

R: Spike Garden

[100 Mana] [110s cd]

Morrigan charms all enemies around her for (1s) and deals [675/880/1050/1280] damage to them. Already charmed enemies take [50%] more damage.
