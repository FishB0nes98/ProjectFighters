Balance Changes

Champions

Kagome

The Spiritual Archer

  • Stats:
    • AD Growth decreased to 10 from 12.
  • W - Soul Arrows:
    • Cooldown increased to 9/8/7/7/6s from 7/6/5/5/4s.
    • Mana cost increased to 90 from 75.

Developer Comment: Kagome's damage output and ability to spam Soul Arrows was too high after the previous patch. These changes aim to reduce her overall damage scaling and make her W ability more manageable to play against.

Lili

The Agile Acrobat

  • Stats:
    • Base Movement Speed increased to 345 from 340.
  • Passive - Agile Acrobat:
    • Now grants 35% tenacity during the duration.
  • Talents:
    • Agile Assault (Level 1): Bonus damage increased to 25% from 20%.
    • Killer Instinct (Level 10): Reworked - Execute Kick now makes Lili untargetable and immune to all damage and crowd control for 2 seconds.
    • Relentless Kick (Level 10): Reworked - Execute Kick now resets all of Lili's other ability cooldowns. If it kills the target, it also resets Execute Kick's cooldown.

Developer Comment: With the upcoming enchanter meta changes, we're giving Lili more tools to survive and thrive. The increased movement speed and tenacity will help her position better and be more resilient to crowd control. The talent changes, particularly to Killer Instinct and Relentless Kick, provide her with powerful defensive options and reset potential, allowing her to maintain her assassin identity while being more effective against peeling supports.

Shoma

The Baseballer Boy

  • Passive - Home Run Points:
    • Grass Ball ally heal reduced to 14% MAXHP from 16% MAXHP
  • E - Swing:
    • Ally heal reduced to 35/120/200/300/385 from 60/180/255/400/500
  • Talents:
    • MVP (Level 1): Increased bonus per point to 2% from 1%
    • Cheering Squad (Level 5): Reduced heal to 3% MAXHP from 6% MAXHP
    • Home Run Hero (Level 20): Reduced shield to 25% max HP from 32% max HP

Developer Comment: With upcoming enchanter item buffs, we're making some targeted adjustments to Shoma's healing and shielding values. The Grass Ball heal reduction is particularly important as it scales very well with healing power. We're also adjusting some of his talents to maintain balance while keeping his core playstyle intact. The MVP talent buff helps compensate for some of these nerfs while encouraging active play.

Alice

The Agile Rabbit

  • Passive - Magic Shield:
    • Magic shield per instance increased to 3% from 2%
  • Q - Charged Earthquake:
    • Now grants 15% damage reduction while charging
  • E - Bunny Bounce:
    • Cooldown reduced to 18/15/13/11/10s per charge from 20/17/17/15/14s
  • Talents:
    • Adaptive Defense (Level 1): Physical damage reduction increased to 3% from 2%

Developer Comment: Alice has been struggling in the current meta, particularly against physical damage dealers. These changes aim to improve her survivability by strengthening her passive shield, adding defensive utility to her Q ability, and making her mobility more accessible. The Adaptive Defense talent buff helps her better handle physical damage threats.

Ibuki

The Stealthy Ninja

  • Passive - Mark of the Assassin:
    • Execute threshold reduced to 7% MaxHP from 14% MaxHP
  • E - Shadowstep:
    • Removed 35% movement speed bonus during invisibility

Developer Comment: Ibuki has been dominating the meta with her ability to quickly eliminate targets and escape safely. These changes aim to make her execute threshold more reasonable and reduce her mobility during stealth, giving enemies more counterplay options while maintaining her core assassin identity.

The healing reduction changes on Scorpion Sword and Shinnok's Dark Magic Bubble aim to provide more effective counterplay against healing-heavy compositions. Support items are being tuned to provide more meaningful choices in their builds.
