Abilities

Passive: Crowd Pleaser

Every time R. Mika lands a crowd control ability, she gains a stack of "Hype" up to 5 stacks. Each stack increases her [5%] damage reduction. When she reaches 5 stacks, she gains a [20% MAXHP] shield that absorbs damage for [5] seconds. Hype stacks are lost after [7s] out of combat.

Q: Mic Performance

[90 Mana] [13s cd]

R. Mika taunts all enemies in a cone in front of her for 1.5 seconds, forcing them to attack her.

W: Shooting Peach

[70 Mana] [6s cd]

R. Mika dashes forward a short distance, knocking up every enemy hit for [0.75s] second and dealing [155% AD] physical damage.

E: Tag Team

[75 Mana] [12s cd]

R. Mika marks a partner teammate. She and her partner gains [+50%] MS for [1.85s] and the partner also gains R. Mika’s Hype for the duration.

R: Rainbow Typhoon [2 charge]

[70 Mana] [100s cd/charge]

R. Mika grabs an enemy and she can carry them for [1.5s]. During that, the grabbed enemy can’t act but it is vulnerable. She can’t grab the same enemy within [10s].

Talents

Level 1

  • a. Mic Mastery: Taunted enemies take 10% more damage for the duration.
  • b. Knockout Punch: This ability now scales with 14% of R. Mika’s max HP instead of AD.
  • c. Tag Team Tactics: "Tag Team" now applies to all nearby allies.

Level 5

  • a. Energized Slam: After using "Shooting Peach," R. Mika gains a 15% attack speed boost for 3 seconds.
  • b. Hypeman: Every stack of "Hype" now also increases R. Mika's HP regeneration by 2%.
  • c. Team Spirit: "Tag Team" now gives 10% additional movement speed.

Level 10

  • a. Typhoon Protection: R. Mika can’t be damaged during the duration.
  • b. Thunderous Applause: Grabbed enemies take 10% more damage for the duration.

Level 15

  • a. Crowd Control: "Mic Performance" also slows enemies for the duration.
  • b. More Hype: R. Mika can now stack up to 7 Hype.
  • c. Nadeshiko's Aid: "Tag Team" heals allies for 5% of their max HP over 5 seconds.

Level 20

  • a. Ultimate Showdown: Using "Rainbow Typhoon" reduces the cooldown of all of R. Mika's abilities by 3 seconds.
  • b. Unstoppable Force: Item: R. Mika becomes immune to crowd control effects for 4 seconds.
  • c. Hype Overdrive: Each stack of "Hype" now also increases R. Mika's damage by 5% per stack.
  • d. Nadeshiko's Encore: Using "Tag Team" doubles R. Mika’s movement speed for the duration.
