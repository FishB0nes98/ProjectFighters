Tanya

Tanya joins the roster as a highly mobile AP Assassin designed for the Midlane. She utilizes sand magic and her spear for poke and pick potential, marking targets to amplify her damage. With her Sand Teleport, Desert Shield for survivability, and CC-cleansing Sand Slides, Tanya excels at swiftly engaging and eliminating priority targets before slipping away.
