Champion Balance Changes

Noel

The Dual-Wielding Sharpshooter

  • Q "Revolver Barrage": Damage changed from 35-245 + 8% AD to 30-200 + 7% AD
  • E "Flash Guard": Damage scaling reduced from 45% AD to 40% AD

Zasalamel

The Time Reaper

  • Base Attack Damage: Reduced from 68 to 55
  • Q Ability - "Reap": Damage scaling reduced from 100% AD to 80% AD

Lili

The Kung Fu Phoenix Girl

  • AD Growth: Increased from 10.25 to 12.5
  • Base HP: Increased from 602 to 615
  • Base Movement Speed: Increased from 340 to 350
  • R Ability - Execute Kick: HP threshold increased from 8200 to 9500. Cooldown reduced from 125s to 100s
  • Level 15 Talent - Reckless Jumps: Cooldown refund increased from 25% to 40%
  • Level 1 Talent - Flashing Acrobat: Added "Cleansing debuffs grants 10% damage reduction for 1.5s"

Akuma

Master of the Fist

  • Passive - Oni's Fury: Damage per Q hit increased from +5 to +7
  • AD Growth: Increased from 4 to 5.5
  • Movement Speed Growth: Increased from 4 to 5
  • Level 15 Talent - Quick Misogi: Cooldown reduced from 6s to 4s
  • Level 15 Talent - Shadow Power: Damage boost increased from 20% to 25%

Morrigan

Soul Stealer

  • E - Bat Dash: Added new effect where kills/assists reset the Bat Dash cooldown
  • W - Sharp Shield: Increased base damage from 80-600 to 100-650
