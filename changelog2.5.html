Balance Changes

Champions

Kotal Kahn

The Sun God

  • Q - Sunlight:
    • Healing per stack multiplier decreased to [2/4/7/10/12/15] from [3/6/10/13/15/20].
  • W - Unbreakable Skin:
    • Damage block decreased to 72% from 80%.
    • No longer heals Kotal Kahn after the duration ends.

Developer Comment: Kotal Kahn's ability to sustain himself and his team was significantly outperforming other supports, especially given his inherent tankiness and damage potential as a jungle/support hybrid. These changes aim to reduce his raw healing output from Sunlight and lessen his personal survivability from Unbreakable Skin, requiring more tactical usage and reliance on teammates.

Kagome

The Spiritual Archer

  • Stats:
    • AD Growth increased to 12 from 10.
    • Attack Speed Growth increased to 0.041 from 0.032.
  • W - Soul Arrows:
    • Damage adjusted to 45/90/170/340/430 from 65/110/170/300/390.
    • Cooldown reduced to 7/6/5/5/4 seconds from 9/8/7/7/6 seconds.
    • Damage now ignores armor.
  • R - Anima Scream:
    • Now has a 150% AP scaling on its damage.

Developer Comment: Kagome has been struggling to find her place, especially against tankier compositions. These changes aim to bolster her scaling and sustained damage. Increasing her AD and Attack Speed growth will help her late-game auto-attack presence. Soul Arrows now provides more reliable damage by ignoring armor and having a lower cooldown, though its early-game base damage is slightly reduced to compensate. Adding AP scaling to Anima Scream opens up potential for alternative builds or enhances its impact if she incidentally gets AP from support items or unique builds, though her primary build path is still expected to be AD-focused.

Shinnok

The Fallen Elder God

  • Passive - The Power of the Amulet:
    • AP granted per kill decreased to [30] from [40].
  • E - Bone Grasp:
    • Cooldown increased to [11s/10s/9s/8s/7s] from [7s].

Developer Comment: Shinnok's early game control, especially with Bone Grasp, was too oppressive against assassins. Additionally, his late-game scaling from AP stacking made him overly dominant against melee-heavy compositions, which are prevalent in the current meta. These changes aim to provide more counterplay in the early stages and slightly temper his late-game power spike without fundamentally altering his kit.

Raiden

The Thunder God

  • Q - Lightning Bolt:
    • Base damage increased to [85/170/310/455/700] from [75/160/300/445/700].
  • E - Electric Dash:
    • Shield gain now based on [200%] of damage dealt (was [100%]).
    • Shield duration increased to [2.5s] from [2s].
  • R - Storm's Fury:
    • Damage reduction during channel increased to [55%] from [40%].

Developer Comment: Raiden has been underperforming across all stages of the game, particularly in the current high-damage meta where his need for precise positioning is heavily punished by assassins. These buffs aim to increase his early lane presence with more potent poke, enhance his survivability when using Electric Dash, and make his ultimate, Storm's Fury, a less risky commitment by increasing its damage reduction.

Siegfried

The Brave Knight

  • Passive - Enhanced Healing:
    • Effectiveness reduced to [12%] from [14%].
  • W - Knight's Resolve:
    • Missing HP heal portion increased to [20%] from [17%].
  • R - Blessed Blade:
    • Lifesteal granted increased to [19%] from [10%].

Developer Comment: To reduce Siegfried's reliance on enchanter supports, we're increasing the self-sustain in his kit directly through Knight's Resolve and Blessed Blade. The slight reduction to his passive healing amplification is to balance these direct buffs, shifting his power towards his own abilities rather than external healing sources.
