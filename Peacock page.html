Q: Rapid Shots

[90 mana] [5s cd]

W: Time Trap

[30 Mana] [10s cd]

Peacock throws a trap at the designated nearby location. If an enemy steps on it, they take [200%] increased damage from Peacock and are rooted for [1.2s]. The traps last for [120s].

E: Clockwork Clobber

[90 Mana] [12s cd]

Peacock swings a hammer in the targeted direction, dealing [50/130/285/395/500/615] damage to hit enemies and knocking them back. If a trap is within the knockback range, enemies automatically fall into it.

R: Robodog (2 charge)

[85 Mana] [60s cd]

Peacock sends her robotic dog after the designated enemy, which chases them until it makes contact. Upon contact, it stuns the target for [1.5s] and deals [300/445/600/795] damage. The robotic dog can be destroyed with [3/3/5/5] autoattacks before reaching its target. Only one charge can be used on the same target.
