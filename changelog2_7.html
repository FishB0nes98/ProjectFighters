New Champion: Anna

Anna Williams

The Rocket Artillery

The deadly assassin from the Williams family brings her lethal expertise to Project Fighters. Armed with a massive rocket launcher and her signature cunning, Anna combines explosive firepower with seductive tactics to dominate the battlefield. Her rivalry with her sister Nina has forged her into a merciless combatant.

P
Artillery Momentum

Anna gains 10% bonus movement speed for each enemy hit by abilities for 5.5 seconds.

Q
Aimed Rocket

Calls a rocket from the sky in a circular area. Deals massive damage with bonus damage for direct center hits.

W
Tempting Kiss

Throws a kiss in a crescent shape that charms enemies and makes them take 50% more damage from Anna.

E
Kunai Strike

Throws 6 kunai in a crescent shape, slowing enemies and reducing their armor by 12%.

R
Power Rocket

Fires a massive rocket with infinite range that completely ignores enemy armor. Damage scales with distance traveled.

⚡ ULTIMATE: Global range, armor penetration, scaling damage

Designer Notes

Anna Williams brings the deadly precision of the Williams assassin bloodline to Project Fighters. As a high-damage marksman, she specializes in long-range artillery combat that reflects her cunning and ruthless nature from the Tekken series. Her kit rewards precise positioning and skillshot accuracy, with her Power Rocket serving as one of the most devastating abilities in the game when properly aimed. Players will need to master her seductive combo potential between Tempting Kiss and her explosive abilities.

Origin: Tekken Series
Role: ADC/Marksman
Difficulty: Medium
Damage Type: Physical
Playstyle: Long-range artillery assassin with high skill ceiling

Developer Notes

Straightshot Aimer was providing too much raw damage for its cost while underdelivering on its unique identity as a range-focused item. This rework shifts it toward a more utility-focused approach, emphasizing attack speed and positioning advantages over pure damage output.

Overall Item Balance Philosophy

These item changes target the enchanter item dominance that has been shaping the meta. Support items like Mermaid Essence, Pearl of the Depths, and Scarab Amulet have been too cost-efficient, making healing-focused strategies oppressive. The nerfs to Cactus Staff and Golden Arrow address overperforming hybrid builds, while the Cyber Revolver buff helps pure AD carries compete in the current environment.
