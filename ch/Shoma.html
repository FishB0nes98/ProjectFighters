Shoma is a hot-headed and passionate baseball player from Gorin High School. He is known for his incredible strength and determination, often letting his emotions drive his actions. Shoma's story begins when his older brother, a professional baseball player, is mysteriously attacked and injured, causing him to be unable to play. Determined to find the culprit and seek revenge, Shoma joins forces with his friends Natsu Ayuhara and Roberto Miura. Together, they investigate the series of strange events happening around their school and other institutions. Throughout his journey, Shoma learns the importance of teamwork, friendship, and controlling his temper. Despite his rough exterior, he deeply cares for his friends and will go to great lengths to protect them.
