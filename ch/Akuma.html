Akuma is a character from the Street Fighter series. He is a powerful martial artist who has embraced the dark power of the Satsui no Hado, a force that grants him immense strength but at the cost of his humanity. Akuma is the younger brother of Gouken, Ryu and Ken's master, and he is responsible for Gouken's death. Driven by a desire to become the strongest fighter, Akuma constantly seeks worthy opponents to test his abilities.
