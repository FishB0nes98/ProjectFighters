State of the Game

Tank Presence
Carry Impact

The competitive landscape continues to evolve with some surprising shifts in character performance. Our data shows several notable trends:

  • • Mega Man dominates with a 61% win rate (39W/25L), showing strong carry potential
  • • Nina maintains high performance with a 71% win rate (22W/9L)
  • • Christie excels with a 71% win rate (20W/8L)
  • • Raiden shows impressive results with a 76% win rate (19W/6L)

However, some characters are struggling in the current meta:

  • • Mai significantly underperforms with a 5% win rate (1W/18L)
  • • Noel struggles with a 15% win rate (4W/22L)
  • • Shoma faces difficulties with a 25% win rate (9W/27L)
  • • Sophitia and Talim both show concerning win rates around 30% (13W/31L)

Key Meta Observations:

  • • High mobility carries are dominating the current meta
  • • Tank effectiveness has decreased due to the damage-heavy environment
  • • Characters with strong self-peel abilities show higher success rates
  • • Early game pressure remains crucial for victory

S-Tier Dominance
Mega Man 61% Win Rate (41W/26L) ADC, Spell-based

Mega Man has risen to become the most dominant force in the current meta, showcasing unprecedented success with a 61% win rate. His dominance stems from:

  • Superior poke damage and wave control
  • Strong objective control capabilities
  • Versatile build paths adapting to team needs
  • Safe positioning while maintaining high damage output

Atlantis Raid - The Depths Await

Extreme 4 Characters

Dive into the depths of the ancient city of Atlantis, where corrupted guardians and mythical creatures await. Face the wrath of Shadowfin and his minions in this challenging 6-player raid experience spanning across multiple unique stages.

Stage 1: The Dark Waters

Medium

Face off against Shadowfin, the terror of the deep, while dealing with waves of deadly piranhas. Master the underwater currents and coordinate with your team to survive.

Key Mechanics
  • Piranha Wave at Turn 30
  • Position-based Combat
  • Underwater Current Management
Notable Rewards
Murky Water Vial (70%)
Tidal Charm (50%)
Void Essence (25%)

Stage 2: The Frozen Depths

Hard

Face off against Atlantean Sub Zero and his frozen warriors in a battle of ice and strategy.

Key Mechanics
  • Ice Strike - Heals Sub Zero for 50% of damage dealt
  • Ice Warriors Summoning (Turn 25)
  • Coordinated Ice Attacks
Notable Rewards
Ice Dagger (25%)
Ice Shard (70%)
Ice Flask (90%)

Stage 3: The Assassins' Ambush

Extreme

Face off against four deadly assassins, each with unique abilities and tactics.

Key Mechanics
  • Death Mark - 45% increased damage taken
  • Venomous Blade - Poison DoT
  • Fan of Knives - AoE damage
  • Drain Life - Damage and healing
Notable Rewards
Shadow Essence (40%)
Shadow Dagger (5%)

Stage 4: The Abyssal Depths

Extreme

Challenge the Dark Leviathan, a massive creature with devastating abilities and regenerative powers.

Key Mechanics
  • Abyssal Slam - Increases ability cooldowns
  • Wave Crush - Stacking damage vulnerability
  • Monstrous Scream - Disables random abilities
  • Passive: Abyssal Regeneration (88 HP/turn)
Notable Rewards
Leviathan Scale (35%)
Leviathan's Mist (30%)

Stage 5: The Shadow's Domain

Extreme

Confront Atlantean Zasalamel and his shadow assassins in a battle of attrition.

Key Mechanics
  • Shadow Protection - Boss immune while assassins live
  • Assassin Spawning (Every 6 turns)
  • Coordinated Shadow Attacks
  • Anti-Summoning Mechanics
Notable Rewards
Zasalamel's Scythe (2%)
Cursed Water Vial (100%)

Stage 6: The Final Battle

Impossible

The ultimate showdown against Atlantean Shinnok, where all previous mechanics culminate in an epic battle.

Key Mechanics
  • Dark Bubble Prison - Ability Disabling
  • Anti-Summoning Curse - No companions
  • Maximum Difficulty (5.0)
  • All Previous Stage Modifiers Active

Talim 🌪️ (Nerf)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base Movement Speed: 371 -> 365
    Reducing her early game mobility to create more counterplay opportunities.
  • W: Windride:
    • Autoattack cooldown reduction: 1s -> 0.5s
    Limiting her ability to chain dashes in quick succession.
  • Level 20 Talent:
    • Health restore on kill reduced: 50% -> 35%
    Reducing her snowball potential in late-game teamfights.

Shoma ⚾ (Buff)

  • Base Stats:
    • Attack Speed per Level increased: 0.029 -> 0.3
    Significantly improving his scaling attack speed to be more threatening in extended fights.
  • Ball System:
    • Base Ball stun duration increased: 1s -> 1.5s
    • Grass Ball lifesteal increased: 7% -> 10%
    Enhancing the utility and sustain of his ball mechanics.
  • Q: Dynamic Rhythm:
    • Stun duration decreased: 1.5s -> 1s
  • W: Samba Slam:
    • Reworked: Now has 2 charges
    • Duration: 1.75s
    • Cooldown: 8s per charge
    • Mana cost: 70
    Reworking his W to provide more strategic options and playmaking potential.

Christie 💃 (Buff)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base HP increased: 614 -> 630
    • HP per level increased: 300 -> 305
    Improving her survivability in the current high-damage meta.
  • W: Samba Slam:
    • Knock-up duration increased: 0.75s -> 0.85s
  • E: Go Easy On Me!:
    • Early game damage amp scaling increased: 25%/25%/30%/30%/40%/50% -> 30%/30%/35%/35%/40%/50%
    Enhancing her early game impact and combo potential.
  • Level 5 Talent:
    • Q hit cooldown reduction increased: 0.5s -> 1s

Blanka ⚡ (Buff)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base HP increased: 600 -> 628
    Improving his early game survivability against burst damage.
  • W: Taunting Presence:
    • Cooldown reduced: 16s -> 14s
    • Damage reflection increased: 5% -> 7%
    Enhancing his defensive capabilities and making his W more impactful.

Ibuki 🥷 (Buff)

  • Passive: Mark of the Assassin:
    • Added: 15% movement speed bonus when moving towards marked targets
    • Bonus damage on marked targets increased: 50% -> 65%
    Improving her target acquisition and assassination potential.
  • Level 5 Talent:
    • Basic attack damage bonus increased: 10% -> 15%
    Enhancing her damage output in extended fights.

Sub Zero ❄️ (Nerf)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base Mana reduced: 605 -> 550
    Reducing his lane sustain and forcing more strategic mana management.
  • E: Freeze Bomb:
    • Base damage reduced: 130/320/435/575/820 -> 120/290/395/525/750
    • Bonus damage on rooted targets reduced: 40/100/295/445/610 -> 40/90/265/405/550
    Toning down his burst damage potential while maintaining his CC utility.
