Balance Changes Notice

Due to the extensive item system overhaul in Season 2, we are temporarily pausing champion balance changes to evaluate the impact of the new items on the meta.

No Champion Balance Changes This Patch

We're holding off on champion balance adjustments while we gather data on how the new item system affects overall game balance.

  • Monitoring champion performance with the new item system
  • Collecting player feedback on item interactions
  • Preparing targeted balance updates for Patch 2.1
  • Addressing any critical issues with hotfixes if necessary
  • Establishing a new baseline for future balance decisions

Upcoming Champions

Get ready for new faces joining the battle! Season 2 will introduce powerful new champions in upcoming patches, each with unique abilities and playstyles!

Tanya

A deadly assassin with lightning-fast reflexes and devastating combo potential, Tanya excels at eliminating high-priority targets with precision and speed.

  • Wields her signature dual-bladed weapons with deadly precision
  • Utilizes teleportation abilities to outmaneuver opponents
  • Specializes in high burst damage and quick escapes
  • Excels in 1v1 duels and flanking maneuvers
  • Unique combo system rewards skillful execution

Anna

A versatile fighter with a mix of close-quarters combat expertise and tactical weaponry, Anna brings a balanced kit that can adapt to various situations on the battlefield.

  • Masters both hand-to-hand combat and advanced weaponry
  • Deploys tactical gadgets to control the battlefield
  • Features a stance-switching mechanic for versatile playstyles
  • Excels at medium-range engagements and team fights
  • Ultimate ability unleashes her signature rocket launcher for massive area damage

Both champions will be released during Season 2, with Tanya arriving in Patch 2.1 and Anna following in Patch 2.2.
