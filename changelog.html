State of the Game

Assassin Presence
Match Duration

The competitive landscape has shifted significantly following our previous balance changes. While Shizumaru maintained a strong presence with a 50% win rate, we've seen notable improvements in previously underperforming characters:

  • • Alice's win rate improved from 64% to 68%, solidifying her position
  • • Birdie saw increased play with a win rate rising from 62% to 66%
  • • Raiden emerged as a strong pick with an 85% win rate
  • • Sub Zero's presence grew with a positive win rate shift

However, some champions have struggled to find their footing:

  • • Noel's win rate dropped to 20%, indicating the nerfs may have been too severe
  • • Sophitia's dominance decreased with her win rate falling to 30%
  • • Yugo remains underutilized with a 25% win rate despite recent buffs

Steampunk Event Skins

Sub Zero (Buff)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base mana increased: 280 ➜ 320
    • Base AD increased: 65 ➜ 69
    Improving Sub Zero's early game presence and reducing his mana constraints.
  • E - Ice Clone:
    • Damage increased: 80/120/180/240/320 ➜ 100/150/220/290/380
    Enhancing his zoning potential and ability to control space in teamfights.
  • Level 5 Talent B:
    • Added: Now grants 10% CDR

Shizumaru ⚔️ (Nerf)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base AD reduced: 82 ➜ 77
    Reducing Shizumaru's early game trading power to create more windows of vulnerability.
  • Q - Deflect:
    • Damage reduced: 80/130/190/260/340/420 ➜ 70/110/160/220/290/350
    Toning down his burst damage potential while maintaining his defensive capabilities.
  • W - Umbrella Stance:
    • Cooldown increased: 14s ➜ 16s

Birdie 🔗 (Nerf)

  • W - Chain Grab:
    • Stun duration reduced: 1.5s ➜ 1.2s
    Reducing the lockdown duration to provide more counterplay opportunities.
  • Level 5 Talent C:
    • Stun duration reduced: 1.2s ➜ 0.9s

Yugo 🐺 (Buff)

  • Base Stats:
    • Base HP increased: 680 ➜ 710
    • Base attack speed increased: 0.45 ➜ 0.48
    Improving Yugo's survivability and basic attack trading potential.
  • Q - Wolf Fang:
    • Armor reduction duration increased: 2s ➜ 3s

Scorpion 🦂 (Buff)

  • W - Hellfire Blades:
    • Added 50 bonus damage to all targets
    Enhancing Scorpion's wave clear and team fight presence.
  • E - Open Portal:
    • Cooldown reduced: 35s ➜ 30s
  • Level 5 Talent B:
    • Added 4% lifesteal against monsters

Noel 🎯 (Nerf)

  • Passive:
    • AD scaling reduced: 12% AD ➜ 8% AD
    Reducing Noel's scaling potential to create more balanced late-game scenarios.
  • E - Flash Guard:
    • Cooldown increased: 9s ➜ 11s
  • R - Zero Gun: Fenrir:
    • Healing reduced: 20% ➜ 15% of damage dealt
  • Level 10 Talent A:
    • MAXHP damage reduced: 6% ➜ 4%
