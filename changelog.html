Balance Changes

Sophitia

  • Passive ‚Äì Divine Blessing: Bonus AD reduced from [5/12/20/28/35] to [3/8/14/20/25]
  • W ‚Äì Sting: Slow effect reduced from 80% to 60%
  • Level 1 Holy Warrior: Bonus from passive reduced from 20% to 15% and attack speed bonus reduced from 0.2 to 0.1
Sophitia's early game dominance and ability to control fights was too strong. These changes aim to reduce her early game power while maintaining her scaling potential.

Angel

  • R: Guardian Angel immunity duration reduced from 4s to 3s
  • E: Seraphic Shield block values reduced from [135/270/445/700/810/1050] to [121/243/400/630/729/945]
Angel has been dominating the support role with her strong peeling abilities and immunity grants. These changes aim to create more counterplay opportunities while maintaining her core identity as a protective support.

Raiden

  • E: Electric Dash cooldown increased from 14s to 16s
  • R: Storm's Fury damage reduced from [100% AP] to [80% AP] and damage reduction lowered from [50%] to [40%]
  • Level 10 Talents: "Wrath of the Storm" stun duration reduced to [0.3s] (+80AP) and "Thunder God's Might" now partially penetrates enemy damage reduction by 20% (+80AP)
  • Movement Speed scaling increased from 350 (+2.5/lvl) -> 410 to 350 (+3/lvl) -> 422
Raiden's teamfight presence was too oppressive, particularly with his defensive capabilities and crowd control. These changes aim to maintain his identity as a strong mage while providing more counterplay options.

Jun

  • Q: Divine Retribution bonus movement speed increased from 20% to 25% and duration extended from 2s to 3s
  • W: Counter Strike now grants a 75-unit dash in the direction of the cursor upon successful parry
Jun was struggling in the mobile ADC-heavy meta, particularly in teamfights. These changes provide her with better mobility tools to keep up with modern threats while maintaining her core identity.

Blanka

  • E: Bestial Charge acceleration time reduced from 3.5s to 2.5s
  • Base Movement Speed increased from 348 to 355
Blanka was struggling in the current mobility meta. These changes help him better engage and keep up with mobile champions while maintaining his tank identity.

Birdie

  • Base AD increased from 52 to 56
  • Passive - I Hate Ya now scales with level: 5% -> 5/6/7/8% (at levels 1/6/11/16)
  • W: Chain Slash stun duration increased from 0.75s to 1s
Birdie needed help in the current fighter-dominated meta. These changes improve his trading potential and scaling while providing more reliable crowd control.
