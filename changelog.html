Balance Changes

Kokoro

  • Q – Harmony's Embrace: Heal reduced to [100/230/380/550/680] from [105/250/400/600/750]
  • W – Healing Strikes: Bonus attack speed reduced to [0.15/0.20/0.35/0.40/0.50] from [0.2/0.25/0.4/0.45/0.55]
  • R – Guardian's Shield: Duration reduced to 2s (from 3s)
Balanced to reduce overwhelming support healing and shielding in late-game scenarios.

Ayane

  • Passive – Sting: Bonus damage increased to [55/130/260/350/650] from [50/120/240/330/615]
  • Q – Teleport Blade: Damage increased to [40/98/230/340/480] and cooldown reduced to [14s/12s/9s/8s/7s]
  • E – Backdash: Damage increased to [80/144/311/437/512] with cooldown reduced to 10s (from 12s)
Enhances her execution potential in the current ADC-peeling meta.

Christie

  • HP Growth: Increased by +4 per level (from 295 to 299)
  • R – Dance of Death: Now grants a shield equal to 8% of damage dealt over 6s
Improves her teamfight survivability against peeling.

Yugo

  • R – Beast Rampage (Wolf Form): Bonus attack damage increased to [180/280/350] and adds 15% movement speed for 7s (previously [150/250/300])
Strengthens his impact in teamfights and improves sustain.

Alice

  • Passive – Magic Shield: Now grants a 2% shield that decays over 6s (resets on magic damage) up to a cap of 50%
  • Talent – Unyielding Stance: Shield reduced to 12% of max HP and tenacity decreased to 10% (from 18% and 20%)
Nerfed tankiness to promote balanced champion selection.

Zasalamel

  • Base Movement Speed increased from 360 to 370
A modest mobility boost to aid repositioning before upcoming item changes.
