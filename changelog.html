Balance Changes

Shoma

  • E ability damage reduction removed
  • E ability now heals allies for [60/180/255/400/500] HP
Shoma's E ability was providing too much defensive utility while also dealing significant damage. By removing the damage reduction and adding healing, we're shifting his role to be more supportive of his team while maintaining his identity as a versatile fighter.

Erron Black

  • E (Shotgun Burst) now makes enemies take 15% more damage from Erron Black for 3s
  • Level 1 Talent A damage bonus increased from 3% to 7% when enemy HP is below 45%
  • Level 15 Talent B reworked: Now grants 15% movement speed for 3s when taking damage
Erron Black was underperforming in extended fights and lacked reliable damage amplification. These changes give him more consistent damage output against low-health targets and better mobility options, while adding a new damage amplification mechanic to his Shotgun Burst to reward aggressive play.

Kuma

  • Base Movement Speed increased from 336 to 345
  • E (Roll) cooldown reduced from 21/19/16/15/15s to 17/15/12/11/11s
  • Level 5 Talent B now grants 30% damage reduction for 2s after Roll ends
  • Level 15 Talent C armor reduction increased from 8% to 12%
Kuma was struggling to fulfill his role as an initiator due to mobility issues. These changes improve his ability to engage and disengage while adding more defensive options after initiation. The increased armor reduction helps him remain relevant in the late game.

Noel

  • AD per level reduced from 11.3 to 10 (End-game AD: 341 -> 310)
  • E (Flash Guard) cooldown increased from 11s to 12s
  • Level 15 Talent B shield bonus reduced from +350 to +250
Noel was overperforming in all stages of the game, particularly against assassins. These changes reduce her scaling and defensive capabilities while maintaining her core identity, creating more windows of vulnerability for opponents to capitalize on.

Mega Man

  • Q (Pixel Beam) base damage reduced from [50/110/200/320/415] to [50/100/180/290/380]
  • Q (Pixel Beam) MaxHP damage component removed
  • E (Dimension Jump) mana cost increased from 90 to 100
Mega Man's poke damage was too oppressive, especially against tanks, while having strong mobility and safety. By reducing his Q damage and removing the MaxHP component, we're creating clearer weaknesses while keeping his unique playstyle intact.

Alice

  • Passive now triggers from any magic damage instead of just spells
  • Level 5 Talent C shield increased from 16% to 18% max HP
  • Level 5 Talent C now grants 20% tenacity while shield is active
Alice was particularly vulnerable to damage-over-time effects and had difficulty dealing with crowd control. These changes improve her defensive capabilities against all types of magic damage while adding tenacity to help her better fulfill her role as an anti-magic tank.
