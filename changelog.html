Key Findings

Tank Meta

Tank presence dominates with 40% representation in top tiers, led by Alice and Sophitia's consistent performance.

Mid Lane Impact

Mid lane carries show overwhelming influence with Shizumaru and Shinnok maintaining 55%+ win rates.

Role Imbalance

Assassins and traditional ADCs are struggling to find their place in the current meta.

Sophitia 🛡️ (Nerf)

  • Passive - Divine Blessing:
    • AD bonus reduced: 5/15/25/35/45 ➜ 5/12/20/28/35
    • Mana regeneration reduced: 3/4/5/8/10 ➜ 3/4/5/6/8
    Sophitia's lane dominance and sustain were too strong, making her difficult to counter. These changes aim to create more windows of vulnerability while maintaining her identity as a tank.
  • Q - Holy Strike:
    • Cooldown increased: 10/10/10/9/7s ➜ 12/11/10/9/8s
    • Mana cost increased: 80 ➜ 90
    Increased cooldown and mana costs will make her more vulnerable to aggressive plays and force more strategic ability usage.

Shinnok 🔮 (Nerf)

  • Passive - Power of the Amulet:
    • AP gain per kill reduced: 50 ➜ 40
    Reducing his scaling potential to prevent overwhelming late-game dominance while keeping his identity as a scaling mage.
  • R - Mind Control:
    • Cooldown increased: 150/125/110s ➜ 180/155/130s
    • Duration reduced: 1/2/2.5s ➜ 1/1.5/2s
    Creating more counterplay opportunities against his ultimate ability, particularly for assassin matchups.

Cham Cham 🌿 (Buff)

  • Q - Catch That!:
    • Damage increased: 100/200/300/410/610/775 ➜ 120/220/320/430/630/795
    Improving her jungle clear speed and early game presence.
  • Level 5 Talent b:
    • Lifesteal threshold: 35% HP ➜ 50% HP
    • Lifesteal amount: 2% ➜ 5%
    Enhanced sustain will help her maintain healthy jungle clears and stay relevant in extended fights.
  • Level 1 Talent a:
    • Reworked: Now grants additional 5% lifesteal and 10% attack speed to W
    Providing more consistent power through her W ability for better jungle sustain.

Jun 🦅 (Buff)

  • Passive - Sky Scout:
    • Movement speed bonus: 15/20/20/25/30% ➜ 20/25/30/35/40%
    Improved mobility will allow for better map presence and team fight positioning.
  • E - Shield of Hope:
    • Shield amount: 130/225/390/510/745/1000 ➜ 150/250/420/550/785/1050
    Stronger defensive capabilities to better fulfill her role as a frontline fighter.
  • Level 10 Talent b:
    • Healing: 565/860/1200/1755 ➜ 600/900/1300/1850
    Enhanced team utility through increased healing potential.

Juri 🌀 (Buff)

  • Q - Whirling Kick:
    • Cooldown reduced: 10/10/9/8/8s ➜ 9/9/8/7/7s
    Improved mobility options for better jungle clear and ganking potential.
  • E - Jump Kick:
    • Damage increased: 95/195/340/555/800 ➜ 110/210/360/580/830
    Enhanced damage output to improve her effectiveness against tankier opponents.
  • Level 5 Talent b:
    • Magic damage shield: 40% ➜ 50%
    Better survivability in extended fights and skirmishes.
