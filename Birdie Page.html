Abilities

Passive: I Hate Ya

Birdie deals [6%] more damage to stunned and rooted enemies.

Q: Headbutt

[35 mana] [8s cd]

Birdie's next basic attack stuns the target for [0.5s]. This ability resets Birdie's autoattack.

W: Chain Slash

[70 Mana/2 charge] [10s cd]

2 charges: Birdie swings his chains in front of him, dealing [125/180/200/330/380/400] damage. If both charges hit an enemy before the cooldown resets (3s), the enemy is stunned for [1s].

E: Come ova’ he’ buddey

[85 Mana] [11s cd]

Birdie throws a chain in the targeted direction. If it hits an enemy, the enemy is marked, allowing Birdie to reactivate the ability within [2s] to pull the enemy towards him.

R: Chain Prison

[100 Mana] [135s cd]

Birdie can chain an enemy from afar, rooting them for [2s]. If an enemy is near the rooted target after the 2 seconds, they are also rooted for [2s]. When reactivated, Birdie can pull all rooted targets towards her.
